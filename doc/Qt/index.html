Qt Parallel


Qt Parallel Site : http://qtparallel.sourceforge.net

Qt Parallel is a supercomputing toolkit for Qt.

The author of QtParallel is Brian Lin, who is also an astrophysicist.  QtParallel is used by the author on his own theoretical physics researches such as Oriphase theory, dark matters and Collective Intelligence Operating System(CIOS) for robotic applications.
